Level 01, the second level, were some come directly from real life is a house with 3 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms a kitchen a living room and a toys room .

This level is small, usualy safe with the only entity being entity 2 that sometimes spawn and perfectly stable.

The halls of the house are made of ligth wood, the bedrooms have the same wood floor each of them has a different colored walls and the bathrooms have different flooring with ligth tiles on the walls.

 

Entrance: real life or level 0*

Exits: the outside door (level 02); the front door (level 03); the basement door (level 04); the living room windows (level 05);  the attic trap door (level 06); the bathroom vent (level 07); staying in the toy room closet for 8 seconds (level 08); the pantry door (level 09); Break a door(level 10);Put the code 0011 in the living room's safe(level 11)


Note*level 0 has access to all levels when you enter them the door at level 0 unlocks but that won't ever be refered again for the sake of simplicity.*
